About Us

We specialized in creating delicious cakes using fresh ingredients and bringing out their finest to our dessert selection. We only use the freshest and finest ingredients in our cakes and pastries, including real butter fresh free range eggs, 100% Cow diary whipped cream, and cacao chocolate. Each cake and pastry are handcrafted the moment you order, our handcrafted cakes are delicious works of edible art! And we would like that experience being shared to you and your guests. Right now our delivery is within the Philippines.

Whether you need a birthday cake, graduation cake, anniversary cake, wedding cake, or a cake for another special occasion, we have a cake for every celebration..

Our Differences:

  • Simple: Our cakes are freshly baked specifically for each order
  • Quality: Only the freshest and finest ingredients are used in our cakes and pastries.
  • Local: We support local farms where ever possible.
  • Passion: Our employees from pastry chef to delivery staff are committed in heart
